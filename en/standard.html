Standard

MACS Core v1.0

The MACS Core defines organisational, decision-related and documentation minimum requirements for handling cryptographically secured digital assets. It is addressed to organisations and functions such as governance, risk, compliance, operations and audit. Core v1.0 is formally fixed and content-wise frozen.

MACS is a reference standard and does not constitute advice, certification or a guarantee of regulatory or economic outcomes.

Normative terms

Within MACS, the following terms are used in a normative manner:

  • must: required for MACS conformity
  • should: recommended; deviations must be justified and documented
  • may: optional

Structure of the MACS Core

MACS Core v1.0 comprises the following sections:

  1. Purpose
  2. Definitions
  3. Scope
  4. Guiding principles
  5. Minimum requirements (Core Requirements)
  6. Subject matter of MACS
  7. Further development
