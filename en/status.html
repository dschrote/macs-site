Status

This page documents the current status, the binding version, and the normative scope of the Minimum Acceptable Crypto Standard (MACS). It serves as the central reference for citation and interpretation.

Standard
Minimum Acceptable Crypto Standard (MACS)
Binding version
MACS Core v1.0
Status
formally fixed
Date of fixation
1 February 2026
Languages
German (Master), English (Authoritative Translation)
Normative scope
MACS Core v1.0 is frozen and constitutes the binding reference framework. Changes occur only through publication of a new Core version.
Supplementation
Thematic clarifications and additions are provided exclusively through annexes and do not alter the normative scope of the Core.

Annexes

No annexes have been published at this time. Published annexes will each have their own status and version information.

Archive

Archived versions of earlier documents are available under Downloads. They are provided for traceability and have no normative effect.

Citation note

When referencing MACS, the binding version indicated on this page shall always be used.
