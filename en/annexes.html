Annexes

Annexes serve the purpose of thematic clarification of specific aspects, contextualization of particular use cases, and raising awareness of potential regulatory, organizational or technological implications, without comprehensively regulating or assessing them.

Annexes are not part of the MACS Core and do not alter its scope or applicability. The current status is shown on the Status page.
