Annexes

Annexes provide thematic clarification of specific aspects of the Minimum Acceptable Crypto Standard (MACS), contextualise particular use cases, and raise awareness of potential regulatory, organisational or technological implications, without comprehensively regulating or assessing them.

Annexes are not part of the MACS Core and do not alter its scope or applicability. The binding status of the standard is shown on the Status page.
