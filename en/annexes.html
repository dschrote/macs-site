Annexes

Annexes complement the MACS Core with thematic clarifications, contextual guidance, and notes on specific use cases. They support interpretation and application of the Core without introducing independent normative requirements.

Annexes are not part of the MACS Core and do not alter its scope or applicability. The current status is shown on the Status page.

Nature of annexes

  • Annexes may explain or contextualise existing Core requirements.
  • Annexes may highlight potential regulatory, organisational, or technological implications.
  • Annexes do not introduce independent minimum requirements.

Published annexes

  • No annexes have been published at this time.

Versioning and archive

Annexes are published with versioning. Archived versions are provided exclusively under Downloads to ensure traceability and citeability.
